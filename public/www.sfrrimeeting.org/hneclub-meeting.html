As part of the extra day of Satellite Meetings for the SFRRI meeting, the next meeting of HNE Club is being held on Wednesday, 5 September at Imperial College London. As in previous years, we encourage younger investigators to submit abstracts for either oral or poster communications at this meeting. In addition to the Herman Esterbauer Award Lecture, the scientific programme will also include keynote lectures by four other established researchers in the field of HNE.

Gala Dinner

BOOK NOW!

Places are still available for the Gala dinner. Tickets cost £50 plus VAT @ 20% and can be purchased by emailing customerservice-sfrrimeeting12@elsevier.com.



We are delighted to invite SFRRI delegates and their guests to a Gala Dinner on the evening of Saturday 8th September. This will be held at The Grand Connaught Rooms an iconic and grand London venue situated in the heart of London’s theatre district and surrounded by the buzz of Covent Garden.

We will step back in time to an age of elegance and romance as we begin the evening with a glass of sparkling wine followed by a delicious three course dinner. Remember places are limited so book early to avoid disappointment!

IOS Press

For 25 years IOS Press has been serving the scientific community worldwide. IOS Press publishes about 100 international journals and 120 book titles each year, covering topics ranging from computer science and mathematics to medicine and the natural sciences. Many titles are listed in the most important abstracting databases, such as Thomson Reuters, MEDLINE and SciVerse Scopus.

All IOS Press journals are available online and in print.

Advances in Biomedical Spectroscopy is a book series that complements the journal Biomedical Spectroscopy and Imaging.

2016 Travel Award Recipients

2016

SfRBM’s Junior Awards Committee wishes to congratulate the winners of the Society’s annual Travel Awards. These awards were made available to students and postdocs who applied to attend SfRBM/SFRRI 2016 to present their research. Submitted abstracts were judged using a double blind review process. Ten (10) awards at $1,000 each were conferred to postdoc or student members of SfRBM or SFRRI Regional societies outside of the United States. In addition, ten (10) Travel Awards at $500 each were presented to postdoc and student members in the US. Winners will receive special recognition and their awards at the SfRBM/SFRRI 2016 Awards Banquet in San Francisco.

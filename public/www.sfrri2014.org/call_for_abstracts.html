Instructions for Abstract Preparation
1. Abstracts should be written in English.
2. All the information you submitted will be sent to your registered email address. This confirmation mail includes your assigned abstract number and login ID. All future correspondence concerning your abstract will include the assigned abstract number. In case you do not receive the confirmation email, your abstract may not be registered.
3. Password is case sensitive and must be between 6 to 8 characters. This password is necessary for you to view or edit/revise your abstract. Please make sure to keep notes of your password. For the protection of personal information we CAN NOT deal with any inquiries regarding your lost/ forgotten password.
4. Up to a maximum of 5 facilities can be registered as Affiliated Institutions.
Maximum number of characters for the first author’s institution is 120 and for the other authors’ institutions are 90 per institute.
5. Up to a maximum of 12 persons can be registered as Co-Authors.
6. Please indicate the presenter by checking the box for correspondent author.
7. The abstract title should be no more than 150 input characters including spaces.
(Approximately 25 words)
8. The abstract body should follow the structure of Background, Methods, Results, Conclusion, and should be no more than 1400 input characters. (Approximately 200 words).
9. The total of the abstract including authors’ names and institutions should be no more than 2230 input characters.
10. Abstracts should not include any diagrams, charts or reference citations.
11. Please paste text processed off-line into the designated box on the Submission Form.
12. Follow the instructions on the Submission Form regarding use of specific character symbols, Greek letters, superscript, subscript, italic, bold or underlined text. You can copy the code on the Submission Form and paste (insert) it as appropriate part of your abstract.
